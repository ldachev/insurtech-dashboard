Methodology

KPI Definitions & Source Notes

Financial KPIs

Revenue is the latest annual 10-K revenue fact available in SEC companyfacts, reported in millions.

Revenue Growth is latest annual revenue divided by prior-year annual revenue, minus one.

Net Margin is net income divided by revenue. ROA is net income divided by total assets.

Capital & Efficiency

Debt/Equity uses available debt facts when reported and falls back to total liabilities when debt tags are not consistently available.

OpEx Ratio is operating expense, costs and expenses, or insurance benefits/losses and expenses divided by revenue, depending on the SEC taxonomy used by each filer.

Radar Scores normalize each metric from 0 to 100 across the peer set; lower OpEx is scored as better efficiency.

Data Source

The dashboard uses the SEC EDGAR companyfacts API and latest annual 10-K facts for the six public peers.

Each company deep dive includes a direct SEC EDGAR link for filing verification.
